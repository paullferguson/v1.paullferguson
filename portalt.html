Project Description

For my final year the boundaries were dropped and I was allowed to pursue an idea of my own. The brief came from my own experiences as a designer where space was always at a premium and the concept of the fully integrated computer was yet to be embraced. The Alt Digital Canvas is a multifunctional design portal, for hand drawing as one would on a conventional desk, a highly productive tablet computer, wither it be CAD or editing and a high quality interactive presentational device in the form of a large screen
