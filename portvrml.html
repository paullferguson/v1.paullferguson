Project Description

Virtual Reality systems involved the production of a interactive VRML environment as well as an animated fly through. Modeled in 3DS Max with textures created in Photoshop the environment contained two interactive Quasi models also produced in Photoshop. The environment was completely re-modeled in detail including rooms behind glass windows and intricate textures.
