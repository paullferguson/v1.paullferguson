Project Description

One of my greatest passions has always been getting out on my bike weather it was racing on my mountain bike or just commuting on my road bike. BMX has always provided me with a wealth of experiences I otherwise would have missed. Through travel and people along the way I have found a lot more than just punctures. A little bit of creativity always makes things more interesting even if one's canves is a bike. So I recently "re-painted" my bike from John Deer green and yellow to "Financial Times."
