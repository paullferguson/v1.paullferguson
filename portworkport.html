Project Description

This was another group project with three product designers, a mech-electronics engineer and a robotics engineer. The brief was to design a simple air compressor, Naturally we decided to push the brief further and ended up developing a complete work unit featuring both a power and air supply, safety cut-out and a work surface clearing method utilizing the compressed air. The branding was developed to get away from the usual industrial approach through the use of colour and softer shape.

Skills Utilized

Analysis of pneumatic systems / strict following of BS standards / Production Planning / Team Management / Power Calculations / Mat Lab / Critical Path Analysis / 3DS Max / Photoshop / InDesign
